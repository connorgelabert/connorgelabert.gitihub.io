Reading

Make Your Bed

William H. McRaven
Takeaway Discipline Habits

I have always been someone who likes things clean and tidy, and I probably get that from my mom. After reading this book, I gained a new appreciation for doing the little things right. Making your bed can feel like a low priority when mornings are rushed and you are just trying to get out the door. Admiral McCraven explains why that simple habit matters. It is the first task of the day, and completing it creates a small win you can build on. Even if the rest of the day goes badly, you still come home to a made bed, which is a simple comfort I know I appreciate.
