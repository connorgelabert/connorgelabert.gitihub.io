Make Your Bed

William H. McRaven
Takeaway
Discipline
Habits

In this book, Admiral McRaven does an incredible job translating his Navy experience into simple lessons that can genuinely improve your life. The most basic, but arguably most important, is the habit of making your bed each morning. By doing it, you complete your first task of the day and create a small win you can build on for the rest of the day.

Maybe it’s because I’ve always liked things neat and tidy, but I try my best to make my bed every morning. It isn’t always easy, especially when I’m rushing out the door. Still, there’s no better feeling than coming home to a made bed. No matter how hard my day was, I can at least come back to that simple comfort.
